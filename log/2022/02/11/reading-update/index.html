An Update On The Reading Situation

11 Feb, 2022

I wrote a post in March last year about my intent of reading a lot more than I’d been doing lately. Consider this a late follow-up :)

After “The Silence of the Lambs”, I spent April reading “Ready Player One” by Ernest Cline — a rather fantastic story that I’d heard a lot about. I enjoyed that very much and subsequently watched the Spielberg film. I enjoyed the film too and didn’t really have a problem with the many changes made (it was a crazy project to film this story in the first place).

In May I read an old favorite of mine - “Watchers” by Dean R. Koontz. I love this story - it’s super suspenseful and hard to put down. Which is also why I managed to cram an extra title into May; I had received the screenplay for my favorite movie “The Prestige” - by Christopher Nolan (the screenplay is written by Christopher and Jonathan Nolan). I’ve read the original book by Christopher Priest as well years ago and it’s another absolute fave of mine.

In June I started reading another Dean R. Koontz thriller - “Midnight”, but I didn’t get to finish it until sometime in July.
